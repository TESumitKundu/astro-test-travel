## Day 1: Arrival in Kochi

Arrive in Kochi, meet your tour coordinator, and transfer to the hotel. Spend the evening around Fort Kochi, Chinese fishing nets, and the old spice market.

## Day 2: Kochi to Munnar

Drive through hill roads toward Munnar. Stop for waterfalls and tea garden viewpoints before checking in at your hill resort.

## Day 3: Munnar Sightseeing

Visit tea estates, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, and local spice shops. The day is planned at an easy pace with time for photography.

## Day 4: Alleppey Houseboat

Travel to Alleppey and board a private houseboat. Cruise through palm-lined canals and enjoy freshly cooked Kerala meals onboard.

## Day 5: Departure

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Kochi airport or railway station.
