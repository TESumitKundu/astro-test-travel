## Day 1: Arrival in Gangtok

Arrive at NJP or Bagdogra and transfer to Gangtok. Evening at MG Marg.

## Day 2: Tsomgo Lake Excursion

Visit Tsomgo Lake and Baba Mandir, subject to weather and permits.

## Day 3: Gangtok Local Sightseeing

Cover monasteries, viewpoints, handicraft centers, and local cafes.

## Day 4: Departure

Transfer back to NJP or Bagdogra.
