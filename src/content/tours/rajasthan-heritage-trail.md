## Day 1: Jaipur Arrival

Arrive in Jaipur and check in. Visit local bazaars in the evening.

## Day 2: Jaipur Forts

Explore Amber Fort, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and Hawa Mahal.

## Day 3: Jaipur to Jodhpur

Drive to Jodhpur and enjoy a relaxed evening near the old city.

## Day 4: Mehrangarh Fort

Visit Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and the blue lanes of Jodhpur.

## Day 5: Jodhpur to Udaipur

Travel to Udaipur with scenic stops on the way.

## Day 6: Udaipur Lakes

Explore City Palace, Lake Pichola, Saheliyon Ki Bari, and local markets.

## Day 7: Departure

Transfer to Udaipur airport or railway station.
