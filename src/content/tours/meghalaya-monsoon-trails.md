## Day 1: Guwahati to Shillong

Arrive at Guwahati and transfer to Shillong with a stop at Umiam Lake.

## Day 2: Shillong Local

Visit viewpoints, museums, cafes, and markets around Shillong.

## Day 3: Cherrapunji

Explore waterfalls, caves, and misty valley viewpoints.

## Day 4: Root Bridge Walk

Take a guided walk to a living root bridge depending on fitness and weather.

## Day 5: Dawki and Mawlynnong

Visit clear-water river views, village trails, and local viewpoints.

## Day 6: Departure

Transfer to Guwahati for departure.
