## Day 1: Arrival in Leh

Arrive in Leh and rest for acclimatization.

## Day 2: Leh Local

Visit Shanti Stupa, Leh Palace, monasteries, and local markets.

## Day 3: Sham Valley

Explore Magnetic Hill, Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, and the Indus-Zanskar confluence.

## Day 4: Nubra Valley

Drive across Khardung La to Nubra Valley.

## Day 5: Nubra Exploration

Visit Diskit Monastery and Hunder dunes.

## Day 6: Pangong Lake

Drive to Pangong Lake and enjoy the dramatic high-altitude landscape.

## Day 7: Return to Leh

Return to Leh with scenic stops.

## Day 8: Departure

Transfer to Leh airport.
